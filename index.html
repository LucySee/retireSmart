How the Passive Income Path Works
Automatically calculated from your assets
No extra input needed. The app projects each asset forward year by year using its return rate. At each future age it checks:
Portfolio Value × Return Rate ÷ 12 ≥ Monthly Expense?
The first year this is true = your Passive Retirement Age.
At that point, you live off the returns without ever selling.

The Goal
Never Run Out
Live off the growth, not the savings
📊 How to read the figure?
EPF pays around 5–6% per year (2025: 6.10%). If you save RM 650k by retirement, you only withdraw 4% a year — that's ~RM 2,167 every month — while the remaining 1–2% stays reinvested and keeps growing your fund.

You're never touching the principal. The fund keeps compounding. Done right, you will never run out of money.
💡 In 2015, 68% of EPF members aged 54 had less than RM 50k in savings — far below the basic threshold. Starting early makes the biggest difference.
🧘 Stay Disciplined in Retirement
⚠️ The most common mistake
Most people withdraw EPF as a lump sum at 55 or 60 — and spend it within a few years. Don't be that person.
✅ The smarter way
Set up monthly withdrawals via EPF i-Akaun — online, no branch visit needed. Only take what you need for the month. Leave the rest earning 5–6% inside EPF.
The money you don't touch today keeps working for you tomorrow.

Withdraw only what you need. Let the rest grow. Never run out.

Why Start Now?
Compound growth · Inflation · EPF limits
▼
📈
Compound interest rewards the early starter
RM 500/month from age 25 at 6% grows to ~RM 500k by age 55. Starting at 35 grows to only ~RM 230k. Same contribution, less than half the result.
🏠
Don't rely on EPF alone
EPF covers basics, but inflation erodes purchasing power. A diversified portfolio (FD, stocks, ETF) builds real wealth.
💸
Inflation quietly shrinks your money
At 3% inflation, RM 5,000 today = only RM 2,750 in purchasing power after 20 years.
🎯
The 4% rule as a guide
To safely spend RM 5,000/month in retirement, you need RM 1.5M saved.
🏦
EPF is a floor, not a plan
Official retirement age is 60. EPF unlocks at 55. Retiring before 55? You need non-EPF assets to bridge the gap.
🌱
Small steps compound into freedom
Even RM 200/month from age 25 can grow to ~RM 200k by 55 (6%). You don't need to be rich to start — you need to start to get rich.
